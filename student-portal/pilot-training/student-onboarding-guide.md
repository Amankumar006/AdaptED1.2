# Student Pilot Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to the Enhanced Educational Platform Pilot Program!

Thank you for participating in our pilot program. This guide will help you get started and make the most of your pilot experience.

## Getting Started

### 1. Access the Platform

- **URL:** https://pilot-student.enhanced-edu.com
- **Login:** Use the credentials provided by your pilot coordinator
- **Browser:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)

### 2. Initial Setup

1. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add your learning preferences
   - Set your timezone and language
   - Upload a profile picture (optional)

2. **Take the Learning Style Assessment**
   - This helps personalize your experience
   - Takes about 5 minutes
   - Can be retaken anytime

3. **Explore the Dashboard**
   - Familiarize yourself with the layout
   - Try dragging widgets to customize
   - Check your progress tracking

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Login Problems:**
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try incognito/private browsing mode
- Contact pilot support if issues persist

**Performance Issues:**
- Check internet connection
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Try a different browser
- Report persistent issues

**BuddyAI Not Responding:**
- Refresh the page
- Try rephrasing your question
- Check if the AI service is down (status page)
- Use the escalation feature to contact a teacher

**Content Not Loading:**
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Switch to offline mode if available

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if I encounter a bug?**
A: Report it immediately using the feedback button or email pilot-support@enhanced-edu.com. Include screenshots if possible.

**Q: Can I use the platform on my phone?**
A: Yes! The platform is fully responsive and works on mobile devices. We also have a mobile app in development.

**Q: What happens to my data after the pilot?**
A: Your learning data will be preserved and transferred to the production system. Personal data handling follows our privacy policy.

**Q: Can I invite friends to join?**
A: The pilot is limited to selected participants. However, you can refer friends for future programs.

**Q: What if I can't complete a validation task?**
A: That's okay! Validation tasks are optional and help us improve the platform. Do what you can and provide feedback about any difficulties.

**Q: How often should I provide feedback?**
A: Provide feedback whenever you encounter something noteworthy - good or bad. Weekly feedback is encouraged but not required.
